# Business Model: Community Accommodation Operations

## Classification
- Repository: `cloud-itonami-5510`
- ISIC Rev.5: `5510` — accommodation operations — guest intake, housekeeping, service and checkout for small properties
- Social impact: tourism-access worker-safety local-economy

## Customer
- small hotels, guesthouses and cooperatives leaving closed PMS SaaS

## Offer
- reservation and check-in, housekeeping and service workflows, guest records, checkout and audit

## Revenue
- setup fee per property, monthly operations subscription, booking integration services

## Trust Controls
- guest disclosures require approval; service records cannot be suppressed; guest personal data stays outside Git
- a robot action the governor refuses is never dispatched to hardware
- every dispatch, hold, approval and disclosure path is auditable
- sensitive operating and personal data stays outside Git
